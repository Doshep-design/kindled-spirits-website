Our Mission

We aim to help and raise support for seniors and the elderly in assisted living communities. Our goal is to provide forms of education and entertainment to seniors, as well as raising awareness for geriatric diseases and the potential isolation seniors feel.

How We Show Up

Our Plan

Kindled Spirits blends student energy with genuine care so every visit, fundraiser, and conversation creates more connection across generations.

Provide direct entertainment and education in senior homes through student activities
Raise awareness for diseases and geriatric issues through fundraisers
Help fund and support developments in geriatric technology
